About us

Msternal & Infant Specialist Center

The two founders of C&L Traditional Chinese Medicine come from the field of traditional Chinese medicine, adhering to the concept of focusing on traditional Chinese medicine and keeping pace with the times, hoping to introduce traditional Chinese medicine into modern medicine. C&L Traditional Chinese Medicine focuses on the protection of the health of pregnant women and infants. Its business includes: maternal health center, child massage center, and medicinal diet center.

Maternal health center

Maternal health centers include treatment of fetal malposition, massage to relieve discomfort during pregnancy, postpartum recovery, and insufficient postpartum breast milk.

Child massage center

Massage centers for children include massage treatment for colds, fever, diarrhea, foot varus and improved immunity.

Medicinal diet center

Medicinal diet center includes: pregnant women nutrition collocation, medicinal diet to help pregnant women recover after delivery, and infant diet nutrition collocation.
